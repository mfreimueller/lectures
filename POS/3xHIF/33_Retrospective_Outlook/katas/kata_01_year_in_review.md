# Kata 1: Year in Review

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Concepts | Reflection, self-assessment |
| Difficulty | 1/5 |
| Duration | approx. 15 min |

### Task

Write a personal reflection on the POS year. Answer these questions:
1. What was the most interesting topic you learned?
2. What was the most challenging topic?
3. What was your favorite exercise or kata?
4. What did you learn from the POS-WMC project?
5. What would you like to improve in 4th grade?

### Example Output

```
POS Year Reflection
=====================
Most interesting: JPA and database mapping - I enjoyed designing the ER diagram.
Most challenging: Stream collectors were tricky at first.
Favorite exercise: The Markdown to HTML converter was fun.
Project: I learned a lot about planning and architecture before coding.
Improve: I want to get better at writing tests first (TDD).
```

### Extension

Write a short letter to your future 4th-grade self with advice based on what you learned this year.
